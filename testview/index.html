about grampaw

Grampaw is a unique collection of 5,555 generative art, collectibles NFTs living on the Solana blockchain.

Inspired by 18th to late 19th era, each Grampaw character and other attributes has been highly researched, designed and manually hand-drawn.

The artwork itself consists of 5 groups, which are background, character, facial expression, outfit and accessories. There are more than 200 traits drawn by the artist.

grampaw pawback

It is in our first mind creating this project, to pawback to the people who devoted their time to help all the paws in need. For this reason we want to give some of our sales, to help neighbouring animal shelters. Later on, in form of documentation we’ll report it publicly for the sake of transparency. In the future we want to include our community to do the same, in their own neighbourhood (tbd).
