about grampaw

Grampaw is a unique collection of 5,555 generative art, collectibles NFTs living on the Solana blockchain.

Inspired by 18th to late 19th era, each Grampaw character and other attributes has been highly researched, designed and manually hand-drawn.

The artwork itself consists of 5 groups, which are background, character, facial expression, outfit and accessories. There are more than 200 traits drawn by the artist.
