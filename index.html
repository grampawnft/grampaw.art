about grampaw

Grampaw is a unique collection of 1,555 generative art, collectibles NFTs living on the Solana blockchain.

Inspired by 18th to late 19th century era, each Grampaw character and other attributes has been highly researched, designed and manually hand-drawn.

The artwork itself consists of 5 groups, which are background, character, facial expression, outfit and accessories. There are more than 200 traits drawn by the artist.

grampaw pawback

It is in our first mind from creating this project, is to return the favour for those who devoted their time to helping all the paws in need. For that reason, we would love to give a portion of our sales, to help neighbouring animal shelters. Later on, all the charity activities that we have planned will be reported to the public in the form of documentation for transparency. In the future, we want to involve our community to do the same, in their own neighbourhood area (tbd).
